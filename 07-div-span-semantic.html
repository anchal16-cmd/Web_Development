Common Semantic Tags

Ye <header> tag hai

Usually website ka top part - logo, site name, navbar yaha hota hai

Ye <nav> tag hai

Home | About | Contact

Ye <main> tag hai

Page ka asli/main content yaha aata hai (jo page ka core purpose hai)

Ye <section> tag hai

Ek topic/group ka content - jaise homepage ka "Services" section

Ye <article> tag hai

Ek complete blog post, news story, ya comment - jo standalone samajh aaye

Ye <aside> tag hai

Sidebar jaisa content - related links, ads, extra info

Ye <footer> tag hai

Page ka bottom part - copyright, contact info yaha hota hai
